The Visionary Behind Vanasthali

Founded with a deep-rooted belief in the potential of rural women, Vanasthali has been a beacon of hope since its inception. Our founder envisioned a society where every woman, regardless of her socio-economic background, has access to quality education, healthcare, and equal opportunities to thrive.

Through grassroots initiatives and community-driven approaches, we are breaking barriers and building resilient leaders for tomorrow. It's not just about providing resources; it's about instilling confidence and self-reliance.

Our Journey of Impact

Tracing our steps from a small initiative to a widespread movement for change.

1998

Foundation: Vanasthali was established with a small group of volunteers teaching in 5 villages.

2005

Expansion: Launched vocational training centers and expanded operations to 50+ villages.

2020

Growth Milestone: Reached over 100,000 beneficiaries and established formal partnerships with government schools.
