The Visionary Behind Vanasthali

Nirmala Balwant Purandare

A pioneering force whose life’s work embodied a deep-rooted belief in the potential of rural women and children. As the guiding spirit behind Vanasthali, she envisioned a society where every woman has access to quality education, healthcare, and equal opportunities to thrive.

Through her tireless efforts in rural Pune and across Maharashtra, she championed grassroots initiatives that empowered communities from within. Her work was not just about providing resources, but about nurturing confidence, dignity, and self-reliance among women.

Our Journey of Impact

Tracing the inspiring journey of 45 years of Vanasthali, from its humble beginnings to becoming a beacon of change for rural empowerment.

1981

Foundation: VRDC was established with a small office run within Nirmalatai's home.

2026

Growth Milestone: VRDC's work is now spread across 9 districts of Maharasthra including about 26 talukas and about 100 villages
